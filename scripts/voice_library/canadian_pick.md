# Canadian-Owned Pick Write-Up Examples

This section type is unique to Canada Picks. There is no Wirecutter equivalent.
The voice should follow the same Wirecutter patterns (specific claims, honest
trade-offs, concrete details) but frame the Canadian angle naturally, not as
flag-waving patriotism, but as practical information for Canadian buyers.

---

## Guidelines (no Wirecutter source, this is our own section type)

**Open with the product, not "as a Canadian-owned brand".**
Bad: "As a Canadian-owned company, bObsweep offers the UltraVision..."
Good: "The bObsweep UltraVision is a self-emptying robot vacuum with 13 kPa suction and pet-focused brushes. bObsweep is headquartered in Toronto."

**Mention the Canadian origin as a fact, not a selling point.**
The product should be recommended on its merits. The Canadian origin is bonus context, not the reason for the pick.

**Be honest about trade-offs vs. the top pick.**
If the Canadian pick can't match the top pick on performance, say so directly. The reader trusts you more for it.

**Mention practical Canadian buying details:**
- Where to buy in Canada (retailer + URL)
- Price in CAD
- Whether it ships from within Canada
- Any Canada-specific warranty or support

---

## Template structure

[Product Name] is a [brief product description with 1-2 standout features]. [Brand] is headquartered in [Canadian city].

[1-2 sentences on what it does well, with specific details. Not "great performance" but "13 kPa suction" or "96% smoke reduction in their testing".]

[1 sentence on the main trade-off vs. the top pick. Be direct.]

[1 sentence on Canadian buying details: where to buy, price, shipping.]

---

## Voice notes

- **Same tone as every other pick.** No special enthusiasm just because it's Canadian.
- **Facts over sentiment.** "Headquartered in Toronto" not "proudly Canadian".
- **Product merits first, origin second.** The reader is buying a vacuum, not a flag.
- **Be frank about limitations.** If the product has zero independent reviews, say so: "No major independent reviewers have tested this model, so we're relying on manufacturer specs and owner reports."
